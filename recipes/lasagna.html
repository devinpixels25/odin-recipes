Lasagna

What is Lasagna?

"Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices" -wikipedia

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Noodles
  • water

steps

  1. Cook and drain beef
  2. stir in spaghetti and sauce
  3. add cheeses
  4. Add noodles
  5. Covered bake ~45min
  6. Bake 10min uncovered
